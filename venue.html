Our conference vanue for this year is "Tirana European Youth Capital HQ".

The venue is in a brand new building, inside the city center ring and close to the major city attractions. It is also the place where the team work on implementing activities related to the 'Tirana European Youth Capital' initiate.

The venue features:

  • a main presentation room
  • two workshop rooms (for parallel tracks)
  • lunch and socializing area
  • a very nice bakery in the ground floor of the building for those coffee breaks.

All rooms will feature projectors, ethernet plugs and wifi
